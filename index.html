Common Fraud Schemes to Avoid
📧 Phishing Emails
Fraudulent emails impersonating DOM Bank. We never ask for your password or full card number via email. Check sender address carefully.
📱 Smishing (SMS Fraud)
Fake text messages with urgent links. DOM Bank alerts never include login links. If in doubt, log in directly at dombank.com.
☎️ Vishing (Voice Fraud)
Scammers pose as bank reps and ask you to verify your account. Hang up and call us at 1-800-DOM-BANK using our official number.
💸 Wire Fraud / Impersonation
Urgency-based requests to wire funds to a "safe account." DOM Bank will never instruct you to move funds this way. Stop and call us.
